Ion



Ion drives are kind of like a video game powerup. At first they are ridiculously weak, but over time they get faster and faster. Eventually they can deliver over 10 times as much thrust per kilogram as a conventional chemical rocket. They work by taking an unreactive fuel like Xenon and charging them positively or negatively and firing them out the back of the ship
